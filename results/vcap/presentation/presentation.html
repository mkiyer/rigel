VBEM Clamp Floor Fix

Before vs After — eliminating the absorbing barrier

Problem: SQUAREM acceleration could push component alpha below recovery threshold. With prior ≈ 10⁻⁶, digamma creates an absorbing barrier — components become permanently dead.

Fix: VBEM_CLAMP_FLOOR = 0.1 — keeps all components in the recoverable regime (ψ(0.1) ≈ −10.4, weight ≈ 3×10⁻⁵). Components can still be naturally suppressed by EM, but can recover if they have genuine read support.
